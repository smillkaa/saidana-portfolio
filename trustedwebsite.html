Client's Goal: The client aimed to establish a trusted online environment for users to verify website legitimacy and safety.

DESKTOP

Approach

As the sole designer, my approach was to handle the complete front-end design of Trusted Website. This involved conceptualizing and implementing the website, developing the brand's image, including the creation of the logo and an Instagram profile that reflected the business' mission. A crucial part of the design process was the creation of the Trust Check badge, which visually confirmed the website's security.

02/

Process

During the design process, I focused on achieving a comprehensive balance between aesthetics and functionality. The design aimed to provide easy navigation and an engaging user interface. I ensured that the website was fully responsive, making it accessible and efficient across various devices.
