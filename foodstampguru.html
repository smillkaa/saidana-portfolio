001/GOAL

The Food Stamp Guru project aims to create an accessible guide for food stamp (SNAP) eligibility. Its primary goal is to simplify the complex eligibility rules and encourage users to apply for food stamps to determine their eligibility definitively.

002/APPROACH

Our approach combines branding and web design to establish a trustworthy and user-friendly platform. We focus on crafting a distinct brand identity that conveys trust and reliability. Simultaneously, our team designs a user-centric website that simplifies eligibility rules and offers an inviting starting point for users seeking information. This approach aims to enhance the overall user experience.

003/PROCESS

The project begins with the development of a unique brand identity for Food Stamp Guru, incorporating elements that foster trust. We then design a user-friendly website that simplifies eligibility rules and provides informative content. The website's design is optimized for easy navigation, ensuring users can effortlessly access the information they need. We integrate the newly developed brand identity into the website's design, creating a cohesive and appealing platform. Rigorous testing is conducted to guarantee the website's functionality across various devices and browsers. Overall, the project's success hinges on simplifying the eligibility process, building trust with users, and guiding them toward taking action by applying for food stamps.
