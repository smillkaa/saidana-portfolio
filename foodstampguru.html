001/GOAL

The Food Stamp Guru project aims to create an accessible guide for food stamp (SNAP) eligibility. Its primary goal is to simplify the complex eligibility rules and encourage users to apply for food stamps to determine their eligibility definitively.

002/APPROACH

My approach combines branding and web design to establish a trustworthy and user-friendly platform. I focused on crafting a distinct brand identity that conveys trust and reliability. Simultaneously, I designed a user-centric website that simplifies eligibility rules and offers an inviting starting point for users seeking information.

003/PROCESS

The project begins with the development of a unique brand identity for Food Stamp Guru, incorporating elements that foster trust. I then designed a user-friendly website that simplified eligibility rules and provided informative content. The website's design is optimized for easy navigation, ensuring users can effortlessly access the information they need. I integrated the newly developed brand identity into the website's design, creating a cohesive and appealing platform.
