001/GOAL

The Careful Accounting web design project aims to create a user-friendly platform for calculating Employee Retention Credit (ERC) refunds and exploring funding options through trusted partners.

002/APPROACH

My approach prioritizes a user-centric design that simplifies ERC refund calculations, provides clear information about potential refunds, and integrates partner funding options seamlessly.

003/PROCESS

I started with an intuitive design, created concise content, and integrated partner options. A prominent call to action guides users, and rigorous testing ensures a smooth user experience.
